## mjml-container

```xml
<mjml>
  <mj-body>
    <mj-container>
      <!-- Your email goes here -->
    </mj-container>
  </mj-body>
</mjml>
```

<aside class="notice">
  MJ-Container was MJ-Body in 1.X
</aside>

This is the starting point of your email.
